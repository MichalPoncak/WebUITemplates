Episode 1 ( - Flashback from Africa - )

A story presented as a flashback or memory of a penguin watching TV. The penguin starts on a west coast of Africa and has to collect various items and coins to achieve higher score. The penguin must make a way from Africa all the way through ocean, underwater canals, mountains and North Pole. The primary goal is to collect the keys hidden across the levels and save Percy’s family which is being held by a big Polar bear which is very hungry.

Episode 4 ( - Wild Dream - )

Percy’s dream which appears to be out of place but the worst thing is that Percy doesn’t have the ammunition. A short level where Percy has to avoid enemies and obstacles in order to ….. ‘Wake up’ :)

Episode 5 ( - Bio Planet - )

This time Percy finds himself in a very strange world full of mysterious creatures. The world seems like it is a one gigantic living thing. Percy doesn’t know how he got there or where he is. It all appears alien and Percy has to explore the world in order to find out more.

Episode 7 ( - The Docks - )

Percy on his way to North Pole was attacked by monsters. Luckily he managed to find a cover and he has only one fish to start with. Percy has to use fishes as ammunition an there not a lot of them around. The primary objective is to eliminate threads at all costs.

Episode 6 ( - Cold Mountain - )

Percy is on the way to a cold mountain and the fastest way to get there is to take a shortcut and use portal created by ogre. On the way to portal Percy can see dead bodies lying around which indicates danger. Help Percy to make it through the portal.

Episode 2 ( - Savior - )

In a peaceful cave, father and son were attacked suddenly during night by a dragon which took Percy’s mother. Percy is about to explore the world beyond the great barrier which separates penguin village from the rest of the world. Are you up to the task to save Percy’s mother?

Episode 3 ( - On The Way Home - )

Percy’s son is about to explore another planet as resources on earth become scarce. He found a found a food source where fishes are unlimited. Now he has to explore the world and find a way to make it back to Earth with the resources. Percy’s son will along the way find rare artifacts scattered all around the world and combining them together may result in a powerful weapon.
