DreamTemplate.com offers variety of graphic products for all different purposes. Our products are split into various categories which target webmasters and web developers. We offer website templates, flash website templates, navigation interfaces for websites, icons and much more.

In order to work with our templates you'll need a html editor such as Dreamweaver, Frontpage, Homesite, 1st Page, etc. To modify the graphics, you'll need Adobe Photoshop 7.0 or software that has the ability to open PSD files. Flash products can be edited using Macromedia Flash MX.

4. How often do you add new products?
We try to add new products on a weekly basis.
